Alvar Alto  

Name : Alvar Alto
Year : 1898-1976
Nationality : Finnish

Hugo Alvar Henrik Aalto. Born in Kuortane, Finland, Alvar Aalto studied architecture at the Technical University of Helsinki from 1916 to 1921. Initially worked as an exhibition designer and later turned to architecture and furniture design in 1923 and 1925 respectively.
