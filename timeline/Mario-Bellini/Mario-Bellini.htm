Mario Bellini  

Year : 1902-1981
Nationality : Hungarian

Born in 1935, Mario Bellini studied at the Polytechnic in Milan, where he got his doctorate in 1959. He opened his studio in 1959, and worked frequently for Olivetti since 1963. Many of his designs can be found at the Museum of Modern Art in New York, where in 1987 a special exhibition was held of his work.
