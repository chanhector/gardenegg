Ettore Sottsass  

Year : 1917
Nationality : Austria

He received his degree in 1939 and started his career in Milan in 1947.
Ever since then Sottsass has been involved in research and formal experimentation in a variety of fields: architecture and industrial design, ceramics and jewelry, craftwork and graphics. He has designed objects for major Italian firms, such as Olivetti, where he has headed computer design since 1957.
