Mezzadro seat, 1971 (1957)  

In this new composition of existing objects, the seat and the crossbar from a tractor designed in the first years of the century and still in production become a seat to be used at home. The fixing screw is the kind familiarly used in bicycles, while a wooden crossbar gives the seat its stability.
