Anna-Castelli-Ferrieri  

Name : Anna Castelli Ferrieri
Year : 1920
Nationality : Italian

Anna Castelli Ferrieri. Italian (1920), Architect and Industrial designer. Art consultant (since 1966) and later art director for Kartell. Several of her own designs, which include plastic shelving, storage systems, modular furniture, kitchen and tableware and stacking storage units were awarded the Compasso d'oro.
